About Us

Pizza Garage Ghosi sits in front of the Cinema Hall, serving stone-baked pizzas with a smoky garage-char flavor. We fire our pies in a traditional stone oven for a perfectly charred crust and bold flavors.

Our Philosophy

We combine garage aesthetic with craft pizza techniques — high heat, simple quality ingredients, and bold tastes. Whether it's classic Margherita or our signature Garage Special, every pizza is stone-baked to order.

  • Hand-stretched dough aged for depth of flavor
  • Locally sourced fresh toppings
  • Open flame + stone deck for char and texture
